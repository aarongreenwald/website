About Me

I'm a software developer in the Washington, DC area. The majority of my work experience has been in the .NET universe, but I've recently begun exploring a FOSS stack for my personal projects. I currently use PostgreSQL and Python (Pyramid) to develop web apps that can be hosted on Linux.

Since I've begun programming, I've worked on a variety of different kinds of projects. Currently, I'm focused primarily on web development. I'm especially interested in how client-side frameworks can be used to make web apps a viable replacement for desktop apps. I've been doing a lot of work with AngularJS recently and I'm starting to believe that although today's client-side frameworks don't eliminate the challenges of developing interactive, desktop-like applications for the web, they're a big step forward for the future of computing. Time will tell, of course.
